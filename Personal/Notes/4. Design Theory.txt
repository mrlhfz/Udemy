# WEB DESIGN

* design is the cheapest ways to make your company/product look expensive

# COLOR THEORY

* be aware of the mood of the colour palatte of your designs
** red - love. energy. intensity. (eg. car ads)
** yellow - joy. intellect. attention. (really attention grabbing: logo/headline)
** green - freshness. safety. growth. (eg. grocery companies)
** blue - stability. trust. serenity. (eg. finance companies)
** purple - royalty. wealth. femininity. (eg. target audience: women)
* how to combine colors
** analogous - 2 colors right next to each other (eg. navbar, body, logo/background)
** complementary - 2 colors on opposite ends of the colour wheel (eg. to make logo pop/stand out, not for text/text background)
** triadic(triangle)
** square
** use adobe color/colorhunt.co for color combo

# TYPOGRAPHY

* serif(feet) - more serious, older, authoritative (mood: traditional, stable, respectable)
* sans serif - more readable (mood: sensible, simple, straightforward) for body texts
* script - (mood: personal, creative, elegant)
* display - (mood: friendly, loud, amusing)
* modern - (mood: stylish, chic, smart)
* only 2 fonts in one design with similar moods and time era
* contrast in serif-ness and font-weights

# USER INTERFACE DESIGN

* bigger/bolder/squarer > smaller/slimeer/rounder to define heirarchy of texts
* color that pop = more important
* layout - different sizes/contrast/shapes to determine heirarchy and make design look more interesting (good layout: grammarly)
* 40-60 characters per line
* lessen number of line of the beginning of content (grid lines)
* white space - empty space around content to make it look more expensive
* design for your audience

# USER EXPERIENCE DESIGN

* design to make things look good / ux what user actually use/need (feels like everything is easy)
* Simplicity
** consistent color scheme/typography
* Consistency
** make design and funtionality consistent (eg. navbar the same in all part of the page)
* Reading Patterns
** F layout - start left (bullet points/pictures/icons) (w/ text content)
** Z layout - (w/ video content)
* All Platform Design
** make web mobile responsive, vice versa
** dont make websites w/ so many banners that make phone versions contents and buttons impossibly small
** TEST, TEST, TEST
* Dont Use Powers For Evil
** dark patterns - get a user to carry out action/behaviour against their wants
